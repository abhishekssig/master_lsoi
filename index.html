About Us

Welcome To LSOI

Lathi Sports ORGINISATION India ,The nation's premier authority on the traditional sport of Lathi. Our headquarters is located in the heart of Delhi, where our journey began, and we have grown into a national organization dedicated to promoting and preserving this ancient Indian art.

fostering its growth at the grassroots level while bringing it to the international stage. We aim to instill a sense of pride in this ancient practice, encouraging people of all ages to participate and preserve our cultural legacy.

On the international stage, we are proudly recognized by the Lathi Sport World Federation, South Asian Lathi Sport Organization, and the Commonwealth Sports Association. These affiliations signify our global presence and our role in promoting Lathi as a sport beyond India's borders. . Our affiliation with Fit India, NITI Aayog (National Institution for Transforming India), and our ISO certification reflect our commitment to excellence and compliance with international standards. We are also registered under the SGFI (School Games Federation of India) and MSME (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises)
